Research & Education

PhD Marine Robotics and AI

Oct 2023 – Present
Scottish Association for Marine Science (SAMS) - UHI
  • Uncertainty-Aware Multimodal Fusion: Developed Bayesian Neural Networks to fuse optical, bathymetric, and side-scan sonar data. Engineered the system to quantify aleatoric and epistemic uncertainty, improving classification accuracy by 8% over unimodal baselines and enabling real-time deployment on onboard GPUs.
  • Cross-Modal Knowledge Distillation: Designed a framework to transfer high-fidelity perception from a "rich" multimodal teacher to an energy-efficient "unimodal" student. Achieved a 5.2% accuracy boost for sonar-only systems, enabling robust perception on resource-constrained robotic platforms.
  • Explainable AI for Autonomy: Integrated SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP) and Random Forests to map model uncertainty to AUV metadata (depth, illumination, navigation). This created a "data-driven" pilot feedback loop for safer autonomous survey planning.
  • Robustness & Physics-Informed Simulation: Quantified the limits of underwater vision by simulating optical degradation. Demonstrated that multimodal fusion maintains 76% accuracy in turbid conditions where unimodal vision models collapse (dropping to 29%).
  • Open Source Contribution: Developed and released the multimodal-AUV library, providing the research community with reproducible codebases and pre-trained models for seabed habitat mapping.
